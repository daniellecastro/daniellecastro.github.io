Audience

Target Audience

The target audience for this site can range from teenager to elderly. The audience is people who want to know about weather conditions. This may be more specifically, outdoors people who are looking for adventure, parents who want to make sure their kids are dressed appropriately for the weather, or anyone planning to travel and need to know what the weather wil be like on their trip.
